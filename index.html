About NTL

Building connections across international markets.

NTL is an international trading and distribution company headquartered in Belgium, with a strategic presence in Türkiye.

We source, trade and distribute goods across international markets, connecting reliable suppliers with businesses and customers worldwide.

We believe that successful international trade is built on strong relationships, reliable sourcing and a clear understanding of the markets we connect.
